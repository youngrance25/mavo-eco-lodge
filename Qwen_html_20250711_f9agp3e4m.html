About Us

Mavo-Eco Lodge is nestled in the heart of nature, offering serene and sustainable living experiences. Our eco-friendly cabins are built using recycled materials and powered by renewable energy sources. Whether you're here for relaxation or adventure, we ensure your stay leaves a minimal ecological footprint.

Accommodation

We offer a range of comfortable, off-grid accommodations:

  • Eco Cabins: Small, cozy cabins with solar-powered lighting and heating.
  • Treetop Retreats: Elevated treehouses surrounded by forest views.
  • FamCamp Pods: Family-friendly geodesic domes with sleeping space for up to 6 people.
